Pancakes.

Description

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter.

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 30 mins
  • Total: 50 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour.
  • ⅔ cup all-purpose flour.
  • ⅓ cup wheat germ.
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder.
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda.
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon salt.
  • 8 ⅓ tablespoons unsalted butter.
  • 2 ½ cups buttermilk.
  • 2 eggs, beaten.

Steps

  1. Step 1:

    2. In a food processor or in a large bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, white flour, wheat germ or oats, baking powder, baking soda, brown sugar, and salt.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Cut the butter into small pieces with a knife, and add the butter to the flour-mixture. Mix until the mixture has a sand-like consistency.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Make a well in the center of the flour-butter mixture, and add the buttermilk and eggs. Stir until the liquids are fully incorporated.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Heat a frying pan over medium heat and grease the surface with 1 tablespoon of butter or oil. Ladle the batter onto the surface to form 4 inch pancakes. Once bubbles form on the top of the pancakes, flip them over, and cook them on the other side for about 2 minutes.
